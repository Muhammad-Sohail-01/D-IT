Overview

The Insurance Payment Module allows businesses to manage insurance payments seamlessly within Odoo. It extends the sale.order model by introducing insurance-related fields and functionality, enabling tracking, automated calculations, journal entry creation, and integration with existing accounting workflows.

Features

  • Manage insurance companies and related policies effortlessly.
  • Automate calculations for insurance amounts based on fixed values or percentages.
  • Generate and post insurance-related journal entries.
  • User-friendly interface enhancements for managing insurance details in sales orders.
  • Seamless integration with existing accounting and sales workflows.

Dependencies

This module requires the following Odoo modules to function correctly:

  • base
  • account
  • sale
  • sale_management
  • tk_vehicle_management (custom module)

Installation

  1. Clone or Download the Repository.
  2. Place the module folder into your Odoo addons directory.
  3. Restart the Odoo Server:
    4. ./odoo-bin -u all
  4. Navigate to the Apps menu in Odoo, search for Insurance Payment, and click Install.

Usage Guide

Setting Up Insurance Companies

  1. Navigate to Contacts > Create.
  2. Add the insurance company details.
  3. Assign the company to the "Insurance" category.

Managing Insurance Transactions

  1. Go to Sales > Sales Orders and create a new order.
  2. Within the Insurance Details tab:
    • Check the Enable Insurance Invoice box.
    • Select the Insurance Company.
    • Enter the Policy Number.
    • Choose Amount Nature (Fixed or Percentage).
    • Specify the fixed amount or percentage.
  3. Confirm the sales order.

Generating Insurance Journal Entries

  1. From the Sales Order form, click the Ins JV button (in the header).
  2. The system validates the data and creates a journal entry automatically.
  3. You can review the generated entry in Accounting > Journal Entries.

Testing the Journal Entry Flow

Prerequisites

  • Insurance Company Setup: Ensure the insurance company is recorded as a partner with the "Insurance" category.
  • Journal Configuration: Verify that an "Insurance Journal" exists under Accounting > Configuration > Journals.
  • Module Installation: Confirm the module is installed and error-free.

Step-by-Step Testing

  1. Create a Sales Order:
    • Navigate to Sales > Sales Orders > Create.
    • Fill in customer and product details, and complete the Insurance Details tab.
  2. Validate Insurance Details:
    • Verify correct calculations for receivable amounts and insurance percentages.
  3. Generate the Journal Entry:
    • Click the Ins JV button, and review the resulting journal entry under Accounting > Journal Entries.
